About Us

Our Academy

Here at Ruffhouse MMA, we have something for everyone. If you want to get in shape, lose weight, learn self-defense, or perfect your competitive fight game, Ruffhouse MMA can help you. We train professional fighters, amateur competitors, law enforcement, and people just starting off their martial arts journey. Our students learn how to react to real life situations by using efficient techniques and simple movements. Our programs are so efficient that state and local police officers trust us to keep them sharp on the most relevant and up to date self defense strategies.

What We Offer
(Adults 13+):

Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu

Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu curriculum focuses on your ability to use technique, leverage, and dominant body positioning to master your opponent on the ground regardless of their size or strength. This allows the smaller, more deficient person to defend themselves against a much larger opponent. With the knowledge that most physical altercations end up on the ground, BJJ is known as the most effective self-defense system in the world. Why? Because it's all about skill and leverage, not size and strength.

MMA

Our Mixed Martial Arts Program includes access to our BJJ program, Muay Thai program, MMA classes, and strength and conditioning classes. MMA classes are a combination of standing and ground, and have multiple arts in one class including: Kickboxing, classic Boxing, wrestling, Jiu-Jitsu, and grappling. Classes include both striking and grappling, and takedowns. Students become very well rounded, put all ranges together, and learn how to move from one art to another.

Strength & Conditioning (RuffCamp)

Our strength and conditioning class, AKA RuffCamp, is lead by our very own Crystal Hill! If you are looking to get in shape by way of traditional or functional strength training, this is the class for you!

Youth (Ages 5 - 12):

Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu

BJJ for kids is a lot like our program for adults. We teach basic fundamentals of the art with an emphasis on self-defense and anti -bullying. Classes consist of warming up and stretching, followed by technique and drilling. Most of the techniques students learn will be on the ground. Each student gets to work with the instructor during class to ensure proper technique is being applied. Students "roll," or grapple at the end of each class.

Boxing

We never want our kids to have to use these skills in modern life, but every good parent still wants their kids to be safe. That’s why our kid's boxing training is very serious about teaching your kids the skills needed for self-protection in any circumstance, from the neighborhood bully to a stranger on the street (and so much more). But knowing how to defend oneself is only half the battle. Our classes teach your child the respect and self-discipline needed to diffuse a situation without violence whenever possible – as well as the knowledge to know when force is the only way to safety.
